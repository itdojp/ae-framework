### Quality Gate integration
- `node scripts/quality/check-lint-summary.mjs` is called from the development Quality Policy lint gate and compares the regenerated summary against `config/verify-lite-lint-baseline.json`.
- The quality gate runs with `maxErrors=0 / maxWarnings=0` in the development environment, so any increase beyond the Verify Lite baseline is detected immediately.
- The summary JSON (`reports/lint/verify-lite-lint-summary.json`) is regenerated by the script, which keeps the check reproducible even when Verify Lite was not executed beforehand.

#### Baseline update rules
- Update the baseline only when the total count decreases and the focus rules (`no-explicit-any` / `no-unsafe-*`) do not increase.
- If the `unknown` rule delta increases, do not update the baseline until the root cause is addressed first (ignore settings, formatter drift, or similar).
- Record the target PR, before/after counts, and the top three remaining hotspots in this document whenever the baseline changes.
